Difference between Paddings, Margins, and Borders:
Paddings: The inner space between the content and the border of your box.
Margins: The outer space (or lack of space) surrounding the box. It is the space between the border and outer elements.
Borders:The perimeter of the box; a line around the element it is applied to. Essentially, borders represent the outer edge of a document or separate sections within a document from each other
